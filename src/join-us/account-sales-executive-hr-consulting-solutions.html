Who we are

All across the world, the landscape of work is changing. Businesses are adapting to the reality of hybrid remote work. Employees are choosing work environments that support their identity and empower everyone to do their best work. Leaders have never had more on their plate.

In this time of rapid change and deep uncertainty, BOxD is transforming leaders, teams and organizations to meet the moment.

At BOxD, we are building a more equitable world of work. We guide leaders to harness the power of organizational alignment so that they can become fierce competitors to industry giants. We believe this type of competition is good for everyone. We develop organizations and teams where all people are supported to leverage their strengths and do their best work. We believe work should make people's lives richer and improve their well-being.

The gist?

We're a minority- and women-owned consulting firm on a mission to make Organization Design accessible to all companies.

We are intentional about bringing individuals together to build bold and creative solutions for our clients.

We are a team of unique, highly skilled organization design consultants, facilitators, and executive coaches.

Why we need you

With our first season of growth behind us, we're excited to take our business to the next level and deliver even more solutions to clients around the world.

That's why we're seeking an experienced sales professional to join our rapidly growing team of experts. We are focusing on serving the technology, biotechnology, healthcare, and financial services sectors, since we think these areas offer the largest opportunity to create more equitable societies.

What will it feel like to work here?

You'd work alongside our team of seasoned organization design and development consultants, who will be your partners in structuring engagements

You'll have access to an amazing list of BOxD customers who are ready to talk to prospects about how we have transformed their businesses

You will be on the CEO's direct team and will partner closely with her to drive growth.

You'd join a distributed-first team comprised of experts with diverse backgrounds, united by a shared purpose of creating positive change

Why is BOxD a great place for a salesperson?

As our first sales professional, you will co-design the sales process and have ample opportunity to accelerate your growth and build deep relationships with rapidly scaling, mission-aligned companies across industries.

We've done our homework! We have defined our services, identified buyer personas, ideal customer profiles, and developed our pitch.

You have the opportunity to leverage your connections and industry expertise across North America.

You'll have direct engagement with CxO's and SVP's to influence customer requirements, align needs to BOxD solutions, and build business value cases.

You will have a competitive compensation package, with an OTE split between base and variable compensation tied to sales quotas & business objectives.

What is the scope of responsibilities?

Own sales goals by prospecting large and medium enterprises (> 500 employees), with many of our prospects being in the technology, biotechnology / healthcare, and financial services sectors.

Own and manage the account sales process, while coordinating with internal and external stakeholders to respond to prospective customers

Prospect for new opportunities, identify key decision makers, identify and qualify key initiatives, deliver sales presentations.

Schedule and conduct meetings with existing and new accounts through telephone calls, targeted email campaigns, and social platforms.

Create Proposals and Statements of Work, and negotiate Master Service agreements

Lead quarterly targeting, pipeline and planning efforts for the business

Arrange and attend internal/external meetings, conferences and engagements as needed – representing the organization

What is the scope of responsibilities?

You have experience selling Consulting Services to senior executives, a track record of achieving sales goals, and are a self-starter able to lead pursuits.

You value freedom, responsibility and accountability in equal measure because you have the capacity to chart your own path and drive new sales

You have a proven record of building trust with internal stakeholders, since you know how to take something off of the CEO's plate and run with it.

Sales or business development experience in Human Resources Consulting including: Organization Design, Team Effectiveness, Leadership Development, Executive Coaching Assessments, Culture Transformations, Diversity-Equity-Inclusion Strategies

Ability to prioritize multiple client relationships simultaneously with conflicting deadlines

Available to travel when needed to meet with our customers

A passion for building something new, curiosity for doing things in new ways, and comfort learning from your mistakes

HR consulting experience/background

HR practitioner experience in our solutions

Sales leadership experience in helping startups scale growth

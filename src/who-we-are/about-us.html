Building a more equitable world of work

On average, most of us will spend over a third of our waking lives at work. That’s why at BOxD, we have this radical idea that work should -- at the very least -- enrich people’s lives and improve their wellbeing. BOxD was born so that we could reimagine what the workplace can look, feel and sound like at its best.

We know that when people work at the intersection of their passion and strengths for a meaningful purpose, this is the sweet spot for everyone. That’s why we care about working with people and companies that are committed to making a positive impact on the communities they serve and operate in.

Our Mission

We make companies their competitive best by transforming their leaders, teams and organizations.

Our Vision

BOxD is working to build a more equitable world in two key ways:

  1. We guide the leaders of companies we believe in to harness the power of organizational alignment and become fierce competitors to industry giants. We believe this type of competition is good for consumers around the world.
  2. We develop organizations and teams where all people are supported to leverage their strengths and be at their best. We believe work should enrich people's lives and improve their well-being.

Our Guiding Principles:

1. We do what's right for our clients, not what's easy.
We anchor our solutions on proven methods, but we flex to your business needs. We are here to help you create sustainable solutions--and we know that handholding is not the same as empowerment. If your project is not a fit for our skills or services, we will refer you to another trusted partner.

2. We leave things better than we found them.
Every BOxD team member is driven by a deep desire for positive change. Our goal in every interaction--even a brief phone call--is to expand our collective thinking, make progress on plans, and build client readiness.

3. We take self-improvement seriously.
We prioritize growth and continuous learning. Our learning never stops--and we don't want it to. We dedicate time for reflection after every project or milestone. We see every client engagement as an opportunity for deep, mutual learning.

Who We Are

The simple answer? We're consultants. BOxD is a minority- and woman-owned consulting firm on a mission to make organizational design accessible to all companies.

We are a team of unique, highly skilled organization design consultants, facilitators, and executive coaches.

We are intentional about bringing individuals together to build bold and creative solutions for our clients.

We're developing teams and organizations where everyone is supported to leverage strengths and do their best work.

We guide leaders to harness the power of organizational alignment so their business can become fierce competitors to industry giants. We know this type of competition is good for everyone.

"Business is personal. You work hard when you care hard. We, at BOxD, can get your people to the intersection of their personal values, unique strengths and a keen sense of purpose to unleash previously unimaginable results."

Nenuca Syquia, CEO

Are we kindred spirits? Let’s find a better way to work, together.

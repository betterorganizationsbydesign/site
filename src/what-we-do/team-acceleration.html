Transform groups of people in high-performing teams

We know there is no one-size-fits-all approach to designing a high-performing team. But we also know that strong teams have a few key things in common: shared purpose, clear roles aligned to people's strengths, and the ability to embrace healthy conflict and diverse perspectives.

Tailored workshops to accelerate the performance of:

  • New teams
  • Distributed teams
  • New or evolving leadership teams
  • Teams with a new leader
  • Teams affected by a reorganization
  • Mission-critical teams
  • Teams with challenging dynamics

How we accelerate your team's performance

Anchored to business outcomes

We never do team building for team building's sake. We build teams to get results, and we do it by getting work done together and having critical conversations. No marshmallow towers or rope courses-- we promise.

Focus on behaviors, not frameworks

We ground our workshops in research, and we keep you focused on shared understanding and behaviors to drive change.

Available online or in-person

We can facilitate your team through workshops in person or online. Our team has over 250 years of combined experience successfully working in and leading distributed teams. Your excellence does not have to be limited by geographic borders.

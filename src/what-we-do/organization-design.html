Match your organizational capabilities to your strategy

Organizational design focuses on aligning the human core of your business with your strategy. Businesses without this alignment tend to suffer from underwhelming business results, inefficiency, dysfunction, and low employee engagement. BOxD can help you redesign your business so that every person and system within your organization are fully aligned to execute on a winning strategy.

When is it critical to invest in your organization's design?

You're evolving your strategy (new focus, products, customers)

Your external environment has changed

You're planning to grow in terms of people, sales, markets or geographies

Your organization is missing performance targets

Your business is merging, acquiring, or is being acquired

You've stepped into a new leadership role and need to have real impact

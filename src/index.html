Why BOxD?

Co-Creation

We never do things TO a client, we do it WITH you. We blend your organizational wisdom with our expertise to build on what already works for you and create high quality solutions that take YOU (not company X) to the next level. Co-creation also allows us to tap into the "IKEA Effect" by embedding change management from the very beginning, instead of adding it as another box to tick at the end.

Enablement

"If you give a person a fish, you feed them for a day. If you teach someone to fish, you feed them for a lifetime."

This is the spirit we bring to every client engagement with BOxD; our goal is to help you strengthen those fishing skills. By partnering with us to co-create outcomes, you’ll walk away with stronger organizational design muscles and sustainable solutions that age well.

Fully Tailored

Working with many other companies and teams has reinforced our belief that every client is unique. We never do cookie-cutter solutions. You don't have time to figure out how something off the shelf can be sliced and stitched to work for your particular context. Everything we design for your organization is always in tune to your organizational culture and fully aligned to your business strategy.
